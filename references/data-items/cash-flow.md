# Cash Flow Statement (`TR.F.*`)

Standardized cash flow statement line items (~49 fields). Use with `ld.get_data()`
and the `Period` parameter. The full list can be regenerated via
`TR.F.CashFlowStatement.fieldname` (see
[../usage/field-discovery.md](../usage/field-discovery.md)).

| Field Code | Title |
|---|---|
| `TR.F.ProfLossStartingLineCF` | Profit/(Loss) - Starting Line - Cash Flow |
| `TR.F.NonCashItemsReconcAdjCF` | Non-cash Items & Reconciliation Adjustments - Cash Flow |
| `TR.F.OthNonCashItemsReconcAdjCF` | Other Non-Cash Items & Reconciliation Adjustments - Cash Flow - to Reconcile |
| `TR.F.DeprDeplAmortInclImpairCF` | Depreciation, Depletion & Amortization including Impairment - Cash Flow - to Reconcile |
| `TR.F.DeprDeplPPECF` | Depreciation & Depletion - Property, Plant & Equipment - Cash Flow - to Reconcile |
| `TR.F.ShrBasedPaymtCF` | Share Based Payments - Cash Flow - to Reconcile |
| `TR.F.CashFlowOpBefChgInWkgCap` | Cash Flow from Operating Activities before Changes in Working Capital |
| `TR.F.WkgCapCF` | Working Capital - Increase/(Decrease) - Cash Flow |
| `TR.F.AcctRcvblCF` | Accounts Receivables - Decrease/(Increase) - Cash Flow |
| `TR.F.InvntCF` | Inventories - Decrease/(Increase) - Cash Flow |
| `TR.F.OthAssetsCF` | Other Assets - Decrease/(Increase) - Cash Flow |
| `TR.F.AcctPbleCF` | Accounts Payable - Increase/(Decrease) - Cash Flow |

| `TR.F.CAPEXNetCF` | Capital Expenditures - Net - Cash Flow |

| `TR.F.StockComRepurchRetiredCF` | Stock - Common - Repurchased/Retired - Cash Flow |

| `TR.F.DebtIssuedReducedSTTotCF` | Debt - Issued/(Reduced) - Short-Term - Total - Cash Flow |
| `TR.F.DebtIssuedReducedLTCF` | Debt - Issued/Reduced - Long-Term - Cash Flow |
